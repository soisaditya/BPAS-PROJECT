Prepare Your Dataset

To unlock the full power of BPAS analytics, your dataset should include the following 6 CSV files with the specified columns. Upload them after signing in.

📦 Orders

Required

Core order records with timestamps for purchase, delivery, and estimated delivery tracking.

  • order_id
  • customer_id
  • order_status
  • order_purchase_timestamp
  • order_delivered_customer_date
  • order_estimated_delivery_date

💳 Payments

Required

Transaction-level payment data used for revenue and payment method analytics.

  • order_id
  • payment_type
  • payment_value

🛒 Order Items

Required

Line-item details linking orders to products with pricing for profitability analysis.

  • order_id
  • product_id
  • price

📋 Products

Required

Product catalog with category information for category-level profitability breakdowns.

  • product_id
  • product_category_name

👥 Customers

Required

Customer geography data enabling regional spend analysis and market insights.

  • customer_id
  • customer_state
  • customer_city

⭐ Reviews

Optional

Customer review scores to correlate satisfaction with product pricing and categories.

  • order_id
  • review_score
💡 Tip: Upload your data as 6 separate CSV files or as a single Excel workbook (.xlsx) with one sheet per table. Column names must match exactly. The format is compatible with the Olist E-Commerce dataset from Kaggle.
